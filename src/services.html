I am a CT certified educator (Music: K – 12) with an extensive history of professional activity and a demonstrated dedication to the training of nascent musicians. I take a thorough approach to teaching performance technique that stresses parallel development in harmony and music theory – the goal being to create well-rounded musicians with a high degree of musical literacy and independence. Students in my studio are regular participants in CMEA Regional and All-State festivals, and several of my former students have gone on to pursue careers in music. I am an active performer and conductor and a published composer, and I have served on the board of the Hartford Chapter of the CT State Music Teachers Association and as a judge for competitions and festivals held by music teacher organizations throughout the state. I am currently a member of the Hartford Music Teachers Allians. I teach private lessons in Glastonbury and West Hartford. I teach 30-, 45- and 60-minute lessons, and my rates are competitive. Students in my studio benefit from:

  • regular (2 – 3 per year) salon concerts at my home
  • a professionally recorded year-end recital
  • the ability to participate in area-wide recitals, festivals, competitons and more through the Hartford Music Teachers Association
  • the opportunity to perform chamber music in small ensembles with other students
  • stylistic diversity (classical, jazz, pop & rock, etc.)

I am currently accepting students in the study of saxophone, piano & composition. Please use the contact link for more information or to schedule a lesson.

I specialize in recording live acoustic (i.e. not amplified) concerts in resonant spaces. If you need a high quality audio recording of your performance, I have the equipment and the know-how to create one. I served as an studio engineer at WHRB in Cambridge, MA and I worked part-time as a recording engineer for recitals and concerts at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music. If you need to record a piece for archival or audition purposes, I have access to performances spaces with exceptional acoustics at the Universalist Church where I serve as Director of Music. Please feel free to contact me for more information or to request a price quote. I am happy to provide the names and contact information of former clients for referral purposes.
