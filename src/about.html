Bio

T. L. Hine (b. 1979) is a published composer, multifaceted performer and certified educator currently residing in West Hartford, CT. Mr. Hine’s compositions are available for purchase or rental through this website – either directly from his own imprint or via links to other publishers. As a performer, Mr. Hine is active as a conductor, singer, and multi-instrumentalist. In addition to his work as a performing musician and creative artist, Mr. Hine also provides music-related services including private lessons, piano tuning and repair, transcription/arranging, and remote classical recording. Mr. Hine is the Organist and Director of Music at the Universalist Church of West Hartford and Music Director of the Montessori School of Greater Hartford.
